Welcome To Latinos United

Latino's United is a clinic created by Eric Nguyen, Andres Martinez, Diego Ortega, and Jose Miguel Romero. This website was created to help the Latino community access infirmation regarding HIV. Latinos United will currently start in Richmond and as time progresses will move through out the bay. At Latinos United you will be able to get information on HIV, a proptype we are working on to help combat HIV among the Latino community, and ways to contact us.
